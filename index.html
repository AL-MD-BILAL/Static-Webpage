GUIDELINES FOR FULL PAPER SUBMISSION

  1. Paper should have an abstract of about 300 words including the Objectives, Methodology and Major findings of the research. The maximum limit of keywords is 5.

  2. The title page of manuscript must include title of the manuscript, name(s) of author(s) including the corresponding author, phone numbers, affiliationss and email for correspondence.

  3. The page limit of full paper is upto 6 pages.Full paper must be typed in MS Word in Times New Roman with font size 12 and 1.5-line spacing.

  4. APA 7 style of referencing for citation should be followed to maintain uniformity.

  5. All pages of the paper shall be numbered. The paper must not have published or accepted for publication elsewhere.

  6. Paper submission, reviews and further queries will be handled through ecevince24@gmail.com.
